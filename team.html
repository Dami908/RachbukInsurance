Meet the Team

ADEKUNLE AJAYI

MANAGING DIRECTOR

Kunle holds a Bachelor of Science and Master’s degree in Business Administration. He is a Fellow of the Chartered Insurance Institute of Nigeria, Associate of Chartered Insurance Institute of London, member of Certified Board of Administration and Institute of Credit & Risk Management of Nigeria. He started his insurance career with NEM Insurance Company and later moved on to Liberty Assurance Company as Senior Risk Analyst. He has worked with Elmac Assurance, Industrial & General Insurance as a Branch Manager and rose to the position of Deputy General Manager in Goldlink Insurance Plc. Kunle, who has over 35 years’ experience in the insurance industry is known for his prowess in Risk Analysis, Branch Operations, Research and Business Development, Business Management and Survey, having attended various courses both locally and internationally. He is happily married with lovely kids.

JUSTINA EZINNE AYINDE

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Justina holds a Bsc. honours degree in Mass Communication from the Lagos State University. She is an upwardly mobile, analytical and quick thinking individual with a passion for excellence especially in the area of service delivery. Her experience spreads through various industries including Banking, Health Management Services, Broadcast Media and Insurance. She has worked with International Energy Insurance Plc. where she won several awards due to her commitment to raising the company to higher levels. Her experience in people matters, having coordinated several teams, is also worthy of note hence her membership with the Human Resource Certification Institute. She is also a member of the Chartered Insurance Institute of Nigeria and has over 15 years cognate experience in the Insurance industry. She is happily married with lovely kids.

BABATUNDE ADEDOKUN

DIRECTOR

Tunde holds a B.sc honours and Master’s degree in Agricultural Economics from the University of Agriculture, Abeokuta and University of Ibadan, Oyo State respectively. He is an Associate of the Chartered Insurance Institute of Nigeria (ACIIN) and Nigerian Council of Registered Insurance Brokers (ACRIB). He started his Insurance career with Goldlink Insurance Plc in 1997 as a Marketing Executive and rose to the position of Regional Coordinator before he left. He has over 20 years cognate work experience which spans through Underwriting and Marketing and Administration. He is a Fellow of Institute of Chartered Economists of Nigeria (FCE) and Associate member of the Nigerian Institute of Management (ANIM), National Institute of Marketing of Nigeria (ANIMN) and Association of Certified Commercial Diplomats (ACCD), London. Tunde has attended several management, leadership, marketing and underwriting courses as well as professional conferences within and outside Nigeria in the course of his Insurance Career. He is happily married with lovely kids.

OLALEYE OLUWOLE

DIRECTOR

Wole holds a B.Eng. degree from the prestigious University of Ibadan in the department of Petroleum Engineering. He has from inception after school, been in business. He is the CEO of "TWO VENTURES" into downstream supplies of petroleum products and logistics. Although the youngest of the team, he has over 10 years business experience. He is happily married with lovely kids.

MRS RACHAEL YETUNDE AJAYI

DIRECTOR

Mrs Ajayi holds a BSc honours degree in Geography from the University of Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria. She is currently the Managing Director of Rachbuk Nigeria Limited, a multidimensional organization that is well rooted in the commercial sub sector of the nation’s economy. Mrs Ajayi’s business contacts in all facets are a great source of businesses to Rachbuk Insurance Brokers Limited. She is happily married with lovely kids

MICHAEL ADEBAYO OREKOYA

DIRECTOR

Mr Michael Adebayo Orekoya is a 1994 graduate of Sociology and Anthropology from the Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Osun State. He also holds a Master’s Degree in Sociology from the University of Ibadan, Ibadan, Oyo State. He is an associate member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel Management. He started his professional career at Robert-Johnson Nigeria Limited in 1998 as a Marketing Executive and rose to the position of Head of Corporate Services. He resigned from Robert-Johnson Nigeria Limited in 2003 to become an Executive Director Administration at Micbelen Nigeria Ltd and later left Micbelen Nigeria Ltd to become Managing Director of Oreks Ventures in 2007, a business he manages till date. He has attended both Local and International courses and passionately adds value to businesses where he has interest. He is happily married with lovely kids.
