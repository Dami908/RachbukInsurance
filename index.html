About Our Company

Our Company Been Working Successfully For 11 Years

Rachbuk Insurance Brokers Limited is a 100% Nigerian owned registered company and licensed by National Insurance Commission (NAICOM) to transact business as Insurance Brokers in General and Life Insurance with registration number RBC 804.

The company is also registered with the Nigerian Council of Registered Insurance Brokers (NCRIB) as per NCRIB Act 2003 with registration number RIB 676.
