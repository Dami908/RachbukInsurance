All Risks Insurance

Life is unpredictable, and accidents can happen at any time, whether to your personal belongings or business equipment. ‘All Risks’ Insurance provides comprehensive protection against accidental loss or damage to your valuable items, no matter where they are located. At Rachbuk, we understand the importance of safeguarding your high-value possessions and critical business assets, which is why we offer tailored ‘All Risks’ Insurance solutions to meet your unique needs.

From personal effects like jewelry and cameras to portable business equipment like laptops, our ‘All Risks’ Insurance ensures that you are covered against a wide range of risks, giving you peace of mind and financial security. Whether at home, in the office, or on the go, we have the expertise to provide the right coverage for your valuables.

What We Offer

Personal Effects: Coverage for high-value items such as jewelry, cameras, and fur coats. Protection against accidental loss, theft, or damage, wherever the items are located. Business All Risks : Coverage for expensive and portable business equipment, such as laptops, desktop machines, and other electronic devices. Protection against accidental damage, theft, or loss, whether on your premises or during transit.
