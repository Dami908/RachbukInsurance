Marine & Transit Insurance

In today’s global economy, the movement of goods across borders is essential for businesses of all sizes. However, transporting goods by sea, air, or land comes with inherent risks, including damage, theft, and delays. Marine & Transit Insurance is designed to protect your goods and shipments against these risks, ensuring that your business can operate smoothly and confidently. At Rachbuk, we understand the complexities of logistics and supply chains, and our tailored insurance solutions provide comprehensive coverage for your cargo at every stage of its journey.

Whether you’re importing, exporting, or transporting goods domestically, our Marine & Transit Insurance ensures that your shipments are protected from unforeseen events, giving you peace of mind and financial security. With our expertise and personalized service, we help you navigate the challenges of global trade and secure the right coverage for your business.

What We Offer

Marine Cargo Insurance : Comprehensive coverage for goods transported by sea, including protection against damage, theft, and loss due to perils like storms, collisions, and piracy. Inland Transit Insurance : Protection for goods transported by land, covering risks such as accidents, theft, and damage during transit. Air Freight Insurance : Coverage for goods shipped by air, including protection against damage, loss, and delays caused by unforeseen events. Warehouse-to-Warehouse Coverage : End-to-end protection for your goods from the point of origin to the final destination, including storage and temporary housing during transit. All-Risk Coverage : Protection against a wide range of risks, including natural disasters, accidents, theft, and malicious damage, subject to policy terms and exclusions. Specific Risk Coverage : Tailored solutions for unique risks, such as perishable goods, high-value items, or hazardous materials. Delay in Transit Coverage : Compensation for financial losses caused by delays in the delivery of your goods. Customs Duty Protection : Coverage for customs duties and taxes in case your goods are damaged or lost during transit. Expert Risk Assessment : Our team conducts a thorough analysis of your supply chain to identify potential risks and recommend the most suitable coverage. Claims Assistance : Personalized support to help you navigate the claims process, ensuring a smooth and timely settlement.
